About Marilyn Monroe

Marilyn Monroe (1926–1962) was an American actress, model, and singer. She became one of the most popular sex symbols of the 1950s and early 1960s, known for her roles in films such as Some Like It Hot and Gentlemen Prefer Blondes. Her life was marked by both dazzling success and deep struggles, leaving behind a lasting cultural legacy.

Key Life Events

  1. 1926 – Born in Los Angeles, California.
  2. 1946 – Signed first film contract.
  3. 1953 – Starred in Gentlemen Prefer Blondes.
  4. 1959 – Won Golden Globe for Some Like It Hot.
  5. 1962 – Passed away, remembered as a legend.

Contributions

  • Became an international symbol of Hollywood glamour.
  • Influenced fashion and beauty standards worldwide.
  • Left a legacy of memorable film performances.
